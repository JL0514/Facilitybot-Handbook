Visitor Entry Request & Gatepass

To maintain security, safety, and efficient operations within our facility, all contractors and visitors are required to follow a standardized process when entering the premises or bringing in/out materials and equipment.

The Visitor Entry Request and Gatepass for Delivery and Pull-Out are managed through FacilityBot, our centralized request and tracking system. These requests ensure that proper authorization is granted before any external individual or item is allowed entry or exit.

System Benefits

  • Monitor visitor access to the facility
  • Track deliveries and pull-out of equipment or materials
  • Secure approval from relevant departments
  • Maintain records for audit and compliance purposes

Requestors must complete the appropriate form in FacilityBot by scanning the QR Code using QR Code application or Viber QR itself. You can gain access by providing details such as purpose of visit or delivery, contact information like email address, items for delivery/pull-out, and expected entry/exit times.

Step-by-Step Process

Getting Started

📱 Access FacilityBot

📱 Android Users:

Download QR code reader app, then scan

📱 iPhone/iPad Users:

Open camera app, then scan

  1. After scanning the QR Code, it will redirect you to Agila Subic Bot using Viber application
  2. Select Service Request
  3. Under Service Request → "Create Service Request"
  4. Once you select "Create Service Request", it will direct you to the Online Webpage Form

A. For Visitor Entry Request / Delivery

This is for your Ingress/Entrance. If you need to deliver items, fill up the Gatepass section as well. If none, you may proceed in submitting your request with the following details:

  • Requestor's Name
  • Requestor's Company Name
  • Requestor's Email Address
  • Purpose of Visit (Specify or Elaborate your purpose of visit)
  • List of Names of Visitors
  • Vehicle Type & License Plate (ex. ABC 1245)
  • Expected Date & Time of Arrival / Visit / Entry

B. For Gatepass for Pull-out

This is for your Egress/Exit. You may upload the file on the "Attach the list of equipment to be pull-out (max 10 MB each) - Image file only" or in PDF file.

  • Please input your email below to monitor the approval status
  • Bearer/Requestor
  • Company Name
  • Date / Time
  • Input the Approved Case ID of Gate Pass Delivery - You may input the Case ID of your Ingress so our security can verify
  • Reason of Pull-Out

Submission and Follow-up

  1. Once you fill out the form, select "Submit"
  2. You may follow up your request using the "Case ID"
  3. You may locate your Case ID in your Viber, Agila Subic Bot or after submitting the request
  4. You may also view the status by clicking "View my Request"

Important Notes

  • • Present your Case ID to Security for quick verification
  • • Check the status: pending, processing, or completed
  • • Once completed, our security will verify it

Service Request

To maintain security, safety, and efficient operations, all concerns requiring assistance from the Engineering Team, F&ES, or Security must be formally submitted through our Service Request system in FacilityBot.

System Benefits

By centralizing all requests in one platform, we ensure that:

  • Every request is properly documented with complete details from the requestor
  • Tasks are accurately routed to the appropriate department, avoiding delays or miscommunication
  • All requests are monitored in real time, allowing requestors to track progress and receive timely updates
  • Issues are prioritized based on urgency (Low, Medium, High, or Critical) to ensure that critical matters are addressed immediately
  • Supporting documents such as images or PDFs can be uploaded, providing clearer context for faster resolution
  • A complete record of requests and actions taken is kept for accountability, audit, and future reference

Step-by-Step Process

Getting Started

  1. After scanning the QR Code, it will redirect you to Agila Subic Bot using Viber application
  2. Select Service Request
  3. Under Service Request → "Create Service Request"
  4. Once you select "Create Service Request", it will direct you to the Online Webpage Form

Fill out the form with the following details:

Requestor Information

  • • Name
  • • Contact No.
  • • Company
  • • Email

Request Details

  • • Nature of Work
  • • Details of Concern
  • • Priority Level
  • • Fault Tag

Schedule & Location

  • • Preferred date and time
  • • Exact site or area

Supporting Documents

  • • Photos or PDF files
  • • Additional references

Nature of Work Examples

  • Power Supply - Electrical issues, outages, wiring problems
  • Water Supply - Plumbing, leaks, water pressure issues
  • Lift Station - Elevator maintenance and repairs
  • Support Service - Ambulance, Fire Truck, Training
  • Others - Any other facility-related concerns

  • • Maintain clear records for accountability and audit purposes

Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) Induction

The Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) Induction is a mandatory orientation designed to familiarize all personnel, contractors, and visitors with the safety policies, emergency procedures, and environmental protocols within our facility.

This induction ensures that everyone entering the site understands their responsibilities in maintaining a safe and secure working environment. It aligns with our commitment to uphold the highest standards of occupational health, safety, and environmental protection.

Objectives

Through the HSE Induction, we aim to:

  • Promote a strong safety culture across all levels
  • Minimize risks and prevent workplace incidents
  • Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Protect the well-being of our people, property, and the environment

Mandatory Requirement

All contractors and visitors are required to complete the HSE Induction before performing any activity within the facility.

Step-by-Step Process

Submitting HSE Induction Request

  1. After scanning the QR Code, it will redirect you to Agila Subic Bot using Viber application
  2. Select Service Request
  3. Under Service Request → "Create Service Request"
  4. Once you select "Create Service Request", it will direct you to the Online Webpage Form
  5. Choose HSE Induction and fill up the form
  6. Once you fill out the form, select "Submit"

Important Notice

Please be informed that each request must be submitted individually, as this will serve as the attendance record.

Request Status Meanings

Processing

HSE Personnel has acknowledged the request and will inform you of the induction location.

Completed

The induction has been successfully conducted.

Cancelled

The person did not attend the induction.

Coordination and Scheduling

  1. Once your request has been submitted, an HSE Personnel will contact you to coordinate the schedule and location of your induction
  2. The induction will be based on the date and time you provided in FacilityBot request
  3. Please ensure your contact details and submitted schedule are accurate to avoid delays
  4. You may also view the status by clicking "View my Request"

Tracking Your Request

You can track your HSE Induction request status through:

  • Case ID - Present this to Security for quick verification
  • "View my Request" - Check if your request is pending, processing, or completed
  • Viber notifications - Receive updates through Agila Subic Bot

Who Needs HSE Induction?

  • • All contractors working on-site
  • • Visitors who will stay frequently or for extended periods
  • • Vendors and suppliers with regular facility access
  • • Any personnel performing activities within the facility
